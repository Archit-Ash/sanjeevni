Constipation

Constipation is a condition in which a person has infrequent bowel movements or difficulty passing stools. It is defined as having less than three bowel movements per week. Constipation can also include symptoms such as hard, dry stools, abdominal pain or bloating, and a feeling of incomplete evacuation after a bowel movement.

Home Remedies

  • Warm water and lemon: Start your day with a glass of warm water mixed with the juice of half a lemon. This can help to stimulate your bowels and get things moving.
  • Prune juice: Prunes and prune juice are known to be natural laxatives. Drinking a glass of prune juice in the morning can help to relieve constipation.
  • Apple cider vinegar: Mixing a tablespoon of apple cider vinegar in a glass of water and drinking it before meals may help to stimulate bowel movements.
  • Aloe vera juice: Drinking aloe vera juice can help to relieve constipation by promoting the growth of friendly bacteria in the gut and lubricating the intestines.
  • Chia seeds: Soak chia seeds in water for about 15 minutes. Chia seeds absorb water and swell, creating a gel-like substance that can help to regulate bowel movements.
  • Ground flaxseed: Mixing a tablespoon of ground flaxseed in your cereal, yogurt or smoothie can provide added fiber to your diet which can help with constipation.
