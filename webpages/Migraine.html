Migraine

A migraine is a type of headache characterized by a moderate to severe pain that is often described as a pounding or pulsing sensation. Migraines can also cause other symptoms, such as sensitivity to light and sound, nausea, and vomiting. The pain of a migraine is usually on one side of the head, but can affect both sides. Some people also experience an "aura" before a migraine, which can include visual disturbances such as flashes of light or blind spots.

  • Vacha (Acorus calamus): This herb is believed to help balance the vata and pitta doshas, which can cure migraines.
  • Vidanga (Embelia ribes): This herb is used to balance the vata and kapha doshas, which can cure migraines

Home Remedies

  • Cold compresses: Placing a cold compress on the affected area can help reduce inflammation and pain.
  • Hot compresses: Placing a hot compress on the affected area can help to relax tense muscles and reduce pain.
  • Hydration: Drinking plenty of water can help to prevent dehydration, which can trigger migraines.
  • Sleep: Getting enough sleep and maintaining a regular sleep schedule can help to prevent migraines.
