Osteoarthritis

Osteoarthritis (OA) is a degenerative joint disease that occurs when the cartilage that cushions the joints wears down over time. This leads to pain, stiffness, and loss of movement in the affected joints.

  • Shallaki (Boswellia serrata) : It is an anti-inflammatory agent that can help reduce pain and inflammation in the joints.
  • Nirgundi (Vitex negundo) : This herb is known for its anti-inflammatory and pain-relieving properties and is commonly used in the treatment of OA.
  • Rasna (Pluchea lanceolata) : This herb has anti-inflammatory and pain-relieving properties and is used in the treatment of OA.
  • Ashwagandha (Withania somnifera) : This herb is used to reduce inflammation and pain, and to improve joint mobility.
  • Haritaki (Terminalia chebula) : This herb is used to reduce inflammation and pain and improve joint mobility.
  • Vidanga (Embelia ribes) : This herb is used to reduce inflammation and pain and improve joint mobility.

Home Remedies

  • Heat and Cold Therapy: Applying heat to the affected joints can help to reduce pain and stiffness, while cold therapy can help to reduce inflammation.
  • Weight Management: Maintaining a healthy weight can help to reduce the stress on the joints and alleviate symptoms of OA.
  • Diet: Eating a diet that is high in anti-inflammatory foods such as fruits and vegetables, and low in processed foods, sugar, and saturated fats may help to reduce inflammation.
  • Topical Treatments: Applying creams or gels that contain menthol or capsaicin can help to reduce pain and inflammation.
  • Supplements: Some supplements such as Glucosamine and chondroitin may help to reduce pain and improve joint health.
